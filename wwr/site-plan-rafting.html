Overview

Purpose

The rafting company's goal is to provide customers with a variety of programs and activities in nature and provide a challenging and rewarding outdoor experience. Our mission is to deliver maximum customer security and a commitment to customer service.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
Our target audience is adventurous people and nature enthusiasts looking for a challenging and rewarding outdoor experience.

What do they want?
Typically our clients are in good shape and have a strong sense of adventure. They are looking for a place to challenge themselves and connect with others. the nature.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
On our website, customers will be able to find information about the different rafting trips available, as well as the difficulty levels and safety precautions. You can also find information about the region and what other activities are available.

What are their needs that aren't being met?
Price and available equipment

How will my audience usually access my website (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?
Our website is available through a web browser on all devices (desktop, laptop or mobile)
